About Me

“Careers are a jungle gym, not a ladder.”

― Sheryl Sandberg, Facebook COO & Author of Lean In: Women, Work & the Will to Lead

When interviewing for my first job at various marketing firms, I would frequently get asked the question, "Where do you see yourself in five years?" At that time, I had a definite answer.

But in today's age of technology innovations rapidly changing how we do business and complete everyday tasks, I'm now EXCITED to say I can't say for sure! After starting my career focused on public relations, then working on a wide range of digital and print media, online video and social media projects, to currently working for a mobile app company, I am now proud to be moving into the world of the tech nerd.

I look forward to serving my own company as well as our current customers by helping them integrate and deploy new technologies into their sales and marketing strategies.
